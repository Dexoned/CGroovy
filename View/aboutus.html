About us

Background

C'Groovy is a vibrant, youth-centric music streaming service designed to amplify self-expression through sound. Founded in 2025, the platform was born from a desire to break free from algorithm-heavy playlists and offer listeners a more authentic, emotionally resonant music experience. While global giants dominate the industry, C'Groovy carves its niche by blending technology with culture—highlighting local artists, underground talent, and genre fusion that reflects the energy of Southeast Asia. Built with a modern and intuitive interface powered by real-time personalization, C'Groovy doesn’t just play music—it adapts to your mood, moments, and lifestyle. Whether you’re looking to get hyped for a workout, zone in during study sessions, or unwind after a long day, C'Groovy curates dynamic listening journeys that evolve with you. More than just a music app, C'Groovy is a community. It supports rising musicians with tools for growth—such as analytics dashboards, fan engagement features, and artist-first monetization options. For users, it’s a place to discover new sounds, join listening parties with friends, and share meaningful music moments. Every element of C'Groovy is tuned to resonate emotionally with its audience—through a bold reddish visual identity, cultural integration, and socially-driven experiences. With its focus on regional culture, innovation, and emotional connection, C'Groovy is poised to redefine the way music is discovered, shared, and felt—not just in Indonesia, but across the global stage.
